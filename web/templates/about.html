   AN INTERACTIVE VISUALIZATION OF AMAZON FBA PRODUCT SELECTION FOR FIRST TIME SELLER


   Using data provided by Jungle Scout (16 product categories | 4670 unique products), this visualization shows 4 pointers for first time sellers when it comes to narrowing the search on Amazon product selection.


   About Our Product:

  
  • We defined the 4 pointers (show below tables) and configured them within the diagrams (by the red threshold line).
    • High Demand + Low Competition Monthly Sales >200 Review <50
    High Demand + Bad Marketing Monthly Sales >200 LQS < 5.5
    Good Return on Investment Monthly Sales >200 Net > 15
    Good Demand + Poor Quality Product Monthly Sales >20 Rating<3.7
  • The user is welcomed to use the sliders to define their own pointers so as to find the product they are looking for.
  • The user can click on the category button to get a facet view of each product category (double click on any blank area to get back to the multi category view).
  • The user can click on any product point to observe if this particular product has met with all 4 predefined pointers (double click on any blank area to get back to the multi products view)
  • When the user hovers the mouse over a product point, a tooltip appears to show more details about the product, so as to provide more insights about this product.
